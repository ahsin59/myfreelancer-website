ICT

ICT, or Information Communications Technology, describes the technologies that people use to share information and conversations. Similar to IT (Information Technology), the crux of the landscape is in information. However, with ICT, the focus is on communicating that information, rather than just collecting, storing, or leveraging it. In the new, digital world, ICT doesn’t refer exclusively to telecommunications. Today, we have desktop applications and cloud-based tools that allow for communication via video, instant messaging, and even social networking. Modern business communications have created a massive landscape, where people can connect with each other in virtually any way that they choose.
